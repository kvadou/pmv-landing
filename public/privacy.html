Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

01

Information We Collect

▾

We collect information to provide a better experience for all our golfers. This includes:

  • Account info: Name, email address, and profile photo.
  • Golf data: Scores, rounds, handicap, and courses played.
  • Location data: Real-time GPS for our rangefinder and live group map features. Collected only while the app is actively in use.
  • Camera & Microphone: Used for live streaming and photo/video posts. Activated only when you choose to broadcast or capture media.
  • Device info: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and bug fixes.
  • Push notification tokens: To deliver timely updates about your rounds and group activity.
02

How We Use Your Information

▾

Your data powers the PMV experience. We use it to:

  • Provide core app functionality including live scoring, chat, and streaming.
  • Display your profile to other members within your joined groups.
  • Show your location on the group map (opt-in only, you control this).
  • Send push notifications for group activity, round updates, and bet settlements.
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs.
03

Information Sharing

▾

We do NOT sell your data to third parties. Ever.

Information is shared only with:

  • Your Group Members: Other golfers in groups you've explicitly joined.
  • Supabase: Our secure database and hosting provider.
  • LiveKit: Our video streaming infrastructure partner.
  • Apple: For App Store services, push notifications, and crash reporting.
04

Data Retention

▾
  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time.
  • Golf round statistics may be anonymized and retained for historical course data after account deletion.
05

Your Rights

▾

You have full control over your information:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time.
  • Opt out of real-time location sharing via in-app settings.
  • Disable push notifications through iOS system settings.
  • Request a full export of your personal data and golf history.
06

Children's Privacy

▾

PMV is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a user is under 13, we will delete their account and information immediately.

07

Security

▾

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest.
  • Authentication is handled via Supabase Auth with secure token management.
  • We regularly review and update our security practices.
