Privacy Policy

calendar_today Last updated: March 19, 2026
01

Information We Collect

expand_more

We collect information to provide a better experience for all our golfers. This includes:

  • Account info: Name, email, and profile photo.
  • Golf data: Scores, rounds, handicap, and courses played.
  • Location data: Real-time GPS for our rangefinder and live group maps.
  • Media: Camera and microphone access for streaming and posts.
  • Technical data: Device info and push notification tokens.
02

How We Use Your Information

expand_more

Your data powers the Poor Man's Vegas experience. We use it for:

  • Core app functionality including live scoring, chat, and streaming.
  • Profile visibility to other members within your joined groups.
  • Opt-in location sharing on the interactive group map.
  • Sending essential push notifications about your rounds.
  • General app improvement and bug fixes.
03

Information Sharing

expand_more

Crucial: We do NOT sell your data to third parties.

Information is only shared with:

  • Group Members: Other golfers in your active groups.
  • Supabase: Our secure database and hosting provider.
  • LiveKit: For processing live streaming data.
  • Apple: For App Store services, push notifications, and crash reporting.
04

Data Retention

expand_more

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Upon a deletion request, we will remove your personal identifiers from our systems. Some golf data may be anonymized and kept for historical course statistics.

05

Your Rights

expand_more

You have full control over your information:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data at any time.
  • Opt-out of real-time location sharing in settings.
  • Disable push notifications via iOS system settings.
  • Request a full export of your personal golf data.
06

Children's Privacy

expand_more

PMV is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover a user is under 13, we will delete their information immediately.

07

Security

expand_more

We take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest. We utilize Supabase Auth with secure tokens to ensure only you can access your account.
